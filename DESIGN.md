# Design System: Acrobat Studio

## 1. Overview

**Creative North Star: "The Confident Marquee"**

Acrobat Studio is a marketing surface that earns the viewport. The page is mostly white. Type is enormous, set in Adobe Clean Display Black at 80–96px, and almost every section opens with one short, declarative line. Color is rationed: a near-black ink for buttons and emphasis, a pure white ground, two warm grays for cards and bento panels, a single signature blue (#3b63fb) reserved for "Try it free," and the Adobe red wordmark as a brand anchor. The page is not loud — it is loud-where-it-counts.

The design is motion-led. The hero pins for 220–240vh while a five-column image mosaic converges from a spread state into a tight 8px-gap grid; copy fades up, ScrollTrigger scrubs the timeline, Lenis smooths the wheel. Every section headline reveals through the global Text Animator: lines slide up from a progressive y-offset between 90% and 40% of viewport height, scrubbed directly to scroll, so the page resolves like an elastic snap rather than a linear ride. Cards round at 16px, sections at 32px on the bottom edge, and a sticky pill in the bottom-center morphs into a full pricing modal on tap.

Anti-references: SaaS landing-page clichés (gradient hero with badge soup, abstract illustration, social-proof logo strip immediately under the hero), 8px-radius rectangles, mid-saturation accent palettes, decorative drop shadows on every card. The system rejects ambient prettiness for exact, specific moves.

**Key Characteristics:**
- White ground, near-black ink, surfaces in two warm grays only
- Adobe Clean Display Black at 80–96px on the hero, dropping to 24px for tile titles
- Type sizes are tokens, not clamps — desktop / tablet / mobile redefine the same names
- Pill-shaped CTAs (75px or 999px radius), no rectangular buttons
- 16px is THE radius — cards, mega-nav panel bottom, mosaic tiles, product cards
- 32px rounded bottom on full-bleed sections so the next layer can peek through
- Signature blue (#3b63fb) is for "Try it free" and the plan-trial CTA only — never on chrome
- Scroll-pinned hero (220vh+), scroll-scrubbed text reveals, GSAP + ScrollTrigger + Lenis vendored locally
- Mega-nav opens via a clip-path expansion of the gnav pill — a single shape transforms across states

## 2. Colors

The palette is restrained: white, warm grays, near-black ink, plus one brand blue and one brand red used as named exceptions.

### Primary
- **Ink Dark** (#000000): The default button background. The carousel-dot active state. The mega-nav active filter pill. Used as foreground on light surfaces and as ground on the sticky CTA pill.
- **Near-Black Ink** (#1a1a1a): Body text inside cards and the hover state for `--ink-dark` buttons. The "active product card" background in the hub router. Slightly warmer than pure #000.

### Secondary
- **Trial Blue** (#3b63fb): Reserved for the "Start free trial" CTA and the plan-trial pill inside the pricing modal. Hover deepens to **Trial Blue Hover** (#274dea). This color does not appear on chrome, navigation, links, or eyebrows.
- **Adobe Red** (#eb1000): The Adobe wordmark anchor only. Not a UI accent.

### Tertiary (surfaces)
- **Surface Light** (#f8f8f8): Mega-nav open background. Perks card ground. Plan card ground inside the sticky pill modal.
- **Surface Soft** (#f6f6f6): Editorial bento section backdrop. App carousel card ground. Hub-router card ground.

### Neutral
- **Surface White** (#ffffff): The page ground. The default product-card ground inside the mega-nav. The hero ground.
- **Text Deep Charcoal** (#2c2c2c): Body copy default. The reset's html `color`.
- **Text Mid** (#4a4a4a): Secondary body copy on light grounds. Editorial sub-deck.
- **Text Muted** (#6e6e6e): Eyebrow text on perk and section headers.
- **Ink Divider** (#d0d0d0): Carousel-dot inactive state. Soft borders inside plan cards (`rgba(0,0,0,0.08)` is the alpha-transparent variant).
- **Card Mid Gray** (#c8c8c8): Image placeholder ground inside `.app-card-image`.

### Named Rules
**The One Blue Rule.** The signature blue (#3b63fb) appears on exactly two elements site-wide: the hero "Start free trial" button and the plan-trial pill in the sticky pricing modal. Anywhere else, the primary CTA is ink-dark on white. Spreading the blue across nav, links, or eyebrows kills its function as a "trial" wayfinder.

**The Two-Gray Rule.** Surfaces are #f8f8f8 OR #f6f6f6 — never both in the same section, and never any other gray. The two-gray vocabulary is the ground for cards (#f8f8f8), bento panels (#f6f6f6), and the mega-nav open state (#f8f8f8). Adding a third gray means the system has drifted.

**The Adobe-Red Reservation.** #eb1000 is the wordmark color. It is not a status color, an accent, a hover color, or a button color. It exists once on every page, in the logo, and nowhere else.

## 3. Typography

**Display Font:** Adobe Clean Display (Black 900, Bold 700, Regular 400) with the system fallback chain `system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, sans-serif`.
**Body Font:** Adobe Clean (with Adobe Clean Display as fallback when body weights aren't licensed) and the same system chain.

**Character:** Adobe Clean Display Black at 80–96px is a typographic gesture, not a typeface. It is wide, dense, terminal-cut, with negative letter-spacing tightening the closure. The body face is neutral — Adobe Clean is humanist sans, Helvetica-adjacent but slightly warmer — so it gets out of the headline's way.

### Hierarchy
- **Super** (900, 96px / 0.94 / -0.04em): Reserved. The largest token in the system; used on hub home pages where the headline is THE content. Tablet 72px, mobile 56px.
- **Title 1** (900, 80px / 0.96 / -0.04em): Hero H1 — "With great power comes great productivity." Tablet 56px, mobile 40px.
- **Title 2** (900, 56px / 1.0 / -0.03em): Section H2 — "There's more to Acrobat than Acrobat." / "Work faster. No matter the work." Tablet 40px, mobile 32px.
- **Title 3** (900, 48px / 1.0 / -0.03em): Reserved sub-display, large card titles.
- **Title 4** (900, 24px / 1.0 / -0.02em): Mega-nav use-case card titles. Plan-card name. List-nav heading.
- **Body Large** (400, 20px / 1.2 / 0): Editorial sub-deck. Hero supporting copy when needed. Tablet 18px, mobile 16px.
- **Body Medium** (400, 16px / 1.3 / +0.01em): Default body copy. Hero subcopy. Section decks. Carousel app body.
- **Body Small** (400, 14px / 1.3 / +0.01em): Plan-card billing line, list-nav items, plan features.
- **Eyebrow** (700, 16px / 1.3 / -0.01em): The category cue above section headlines — "PDF & Productivity," "Customer Stories," "Do that with Acrobat." Often paired with a 24×24 product icon at 8px gap.
- **Label** (700, 14px / 1.3 / 0): Nav links, button copy, sign-in button.
- **Caption** (700, 12px / 1.3 / +0.02em): Reserved for metadata; rarely visible.

### Named Rules
**The Black-900 Rule.** Every Title size in the scale is `font-weight: 900`. Adobe Clean Display Black is the only display weight in the system; never substitute Bold or Regular at headline sizes. The negative letter-spacing values are part of the type, not optional polish — strip them and the proportions collapse.

**The Eyebrow-Headline Pair Rule.** Section headlines are always preceded by an Eyebrow line at 16/700/-0.01em in #6e6e6e (warm) or #2c2c2c (high-contrast variants). The eyebrow is the category cue; the headline is the statement. Never start a section with a Title 2 alone.

**The Tightline Rule.** Display sizes (Title 1–4) use line-heights of 0.94–1.0. They are stacked, not airy. Body sizes use 1.2–1.3. There is no overlap.

## 4. Elevation

The system is flat by default. Cards live on tonal layering — `#f6f6f6` and `#f8f8f8` against `#ffffff` — not on shadow. The single canonical shadow is the editorial right-card photo shadow; the sticky-CTA pill carries a small ambient stack so it floats over the page. Frosted glass (backdrop-filter) is the depth tool of choice for the nav pill and mega-nav dim, replacing shadow with optical recession.

### Shadow Vocabulary
- **photo-card** (`box-shadow: 0 4px 100px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.25)`): Editorial right-card image shadow. The single dramatic shadow in the system; used to lift a photographic image off the gray bento backdrop.
- **sticky-pill** (`box-shadow: 0 0 0 0px #000000, 0 8px 32px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.24), 0 2px 8px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.12)`): The morphing CTA pill at the bottom-center. Two-layer ambient + tight contact shadow over a `0px` GSAP-interpolated grow-hint.

### Backdrop Vocabulary
- **nav-pill-glass** (`backdrop-filter: blur(64px); background: rgba(255,255,255,0.51)`): The default nav pill state when scrolled. The pill is invisible at the top; opacity scales in over 0.35s as the user scrolls past 40px.
- **mega-nav-dim** (`backdrop-filter: blur(32px); background: rgba(0,0,0,0.72)`): The scrim behind the mega-nav panel. Appears as the panel clip-path expands.
- **modal-scrim** (`backdrop-filter: blur(12px); background: rgba(0,0,0,0.6)`): Behind the sticky CTA pricing modal.

### Named Rules
**The Glass-Not-Shadow Rule.** Where a typical SaaS site would add a shadow to lift the nav off the page, this system uses a frosted-glass pill instead. The pill is invisible until scrolled, then crossfades opacity to reveal the blur. Never add a `box-shadow` to the gnav.

**The One-Photo-Shadow Rule.** Use `0 4px 100px rgba(0,0,0,0.25)` only on photographic images that need to lift off a colored backdrop. Don't reach for it on cards, buttons, or chrome.

## 5. Components

### Buttons
The system has six button variants. All are pill-shaped (radius 75px or 999px); rectangles are forbidden.

- **Shape:** Always pill — `border-radius: 75px` (most components) or `999px` (sticky-CTA plan buttons). Never rounded-rectangle, never sharp-corner.
- **Primary (`btn-primary`):** Black on white. `height: 48px`, `padding: 0 24px`, `background: #000`, `color: #fff`, label `14/700`. No shadow, no border. Used as the dominant CTA on every section ("Buy now," "Try it free" when the trial-blue isn't called for, "Get Acrobat Pro").
- **Secondary (`btn-secondary`):** Outlined black. `height: 48px`, `padding: 0 24px`, `background: transparent`, `color: #000`, `border: 1.5px solid #000`. Always paired to the right of the primary; never shown alone.
- **Trial (`btn-plan-trial`):** Signature-blue pill. `height: 40px`, `background: #3b63fb`, `color: #fff`, hover deepens to `#274dea` with `opacity: 0.88` transition. Reserved per The One Blue Rule.
- **Ghost-White (`gnav-signin`-style):** Transparent on dark hero, white outline at `border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.85)` plus `backdrop-filter: blur(16.5px)`. Crossfades to dark-outline when scrolled.
- **Filter-pill (`mnav-filter`):** Mega-nav category selector. Default `background: rgba(0,0,0,0.04)`, active `background: #000; color: #fff`, hover `rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`. `height: 40px`, `padding: 0 18px`.
- **Carousel-arrow:** 40×40 round (`border-radius: 50%`), `border: 1.5px solid #000`, transparent ground, hover `rgba(0,0,0,0.06)`.

### Cards
- **App card (`.app-card`):** Carousel app card. `background: #f6f6f6`, `border-radius: 24px`, image placeholder `#c8c8c8` filling the top with `border-radius: 15px 15px 0 0` (note the 1px difference from the outer 16px — intentional inner notch). Body padded 24px. Card width is JS-measured to align with grid column 1.
- **Perk card (`.perk-card`):** 4-up grid. `background: #f8f8f8`, `border-radius: 16px`, `padding: 32px 24px`. Title in body color, body in `#4a4a4a`. No shadow, no border.
- **Product card (mega-nav `.mnav-card`):** `background: #fff`, `border-radius: 16px`, `padding: 16px`, `min-height: 160px`. Hover flips entire card to `background: #1a1a1a`, with name and badges inverting in a single 0.2s ease transition.
- **Use-case card (`.ucnav-card`):** Mega-nav use-cases pane. `background: #fff`, `border-radius: 16px`, `padding: 20px 20px 16px`, `min-height: 340px`. Same dark-flip hover.
- **Plan card (`.plan-card`):** Inside the sticky CTA pricing modal. `background: rgb(248, 248, 248)`, `border-radius: 16px`. Header padded 24/24/0, body padded 20/24/24 with 20px gap, feature list with `border-top: 1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.08)` and per-row bottom borders.
- **Editorial bento card:** Two-card row. Left: large square (892px-flex), full-bleed photo with copy inset 48px, white type. Right: shorter image card (492px-flex) with `box-shadow: 0 4px 100px rgba(0,0,0,0.25)` plus copy below on the gray `#f6f6f6` ground.

### Inputs / Fields
The reference pages do not include a search or form input. When introducing one, follow these defaults to stay in voice: `border-radius: 75px` (pill), `border: 1px solid rgba(0,0,0,0.16)`, `padding: 14px 24px`, body-medium type, focus state `border-color: #1a1a1a` (no glow ring).

### Navigation

**Global Nav (gnav).** A pill at the top of the viewport, fixed. Outer wrapper has 8px padding; the inner pill is `max-width: 1904px`, `height: 64px`, `padding: 0 16px`, `border-radius: 16px`. Default state: invisible — pill opacity 0, links and logo are white over the hero. Scrolled state (`gnav--scrolled`, triggers at >40px scroll): pill crossfades to `rgba(255,255,255,0.51)` with `backdrop-filter: blur(64px)`, links and logo flip to `#000`. The logo swaps between a white wordmark and a black wordmark via display:none (no opacity blend). Sign-In button is a pill-outlined ghost-white CTA matching the gnav state.

**Mega-Nav.** Triggered from the gnav links (Products, Use Cases, Solutions, Learn & Support). The panel uses a clip-path inset that begins matched to the nav-pill geometry (8px inset all sides, only the 64px pill height revealed, 16px corners) and expands via `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` over 0.48s to fill the viewport with `border-radius: 0 0 18px 18px`. Background crossfades from transparent to `#f8f8f8`. Filter list is a column of pills on the left; product grid is 3-up (mega-nav) or 5-up (use-cases) on the right. Cards stagger in by `nth-child` with 0.18s ease, 25ms apart.

**Mobile Nav.** Hamburger toggle slides off-screen on activation; nav overlay sits behind the gnav at z:99 and clip-path-expands from the nav pill shape — same morph metaphor as the mega-nav.

### Hero
- **Mosaic-Convergence Hero (canonical).** Pin-spacer of 220vh; sticky `#hero` height 100vh; absolute image mosaic with 5 columns × 3 rows, each card a square at 20.2vw with 16px radius and `object-fit: cover`. On load, cards reveal via a clip-path ring ripple centered on (col 3, row 1) using Chebyshev distance — Ring 0 (1 tile), Ring 1 (5), Ring 2 (9), staggered 0.1s per ring with 0.5s duration. On scroll, columns converge from spread positions (-178 to +179px x-deltas, -307 to -290px y-deltas, scaled by vw/vh from a 1440×1024 design) to tight (0,0). `scrub: 0.8` lag.
- **Hero text block.** Eyebrow (with 24×24 product icon) → Title 1 → Body Medium → CTA pair. Centered, max-width on h1 = 16ch and on body = 48ch.
- **Hero pill (`#cta-container`).** Floating bottom-center CTA, default 279×56, `background: #000`, `border-radius: 16px`, GSAP-driven morph into the full pricing modal at viewport size.

### Sections
- **Editorial bento ("There's more to X than X").** A two-card row where the left card is a large photographic square (892px flex-basis, full-bleed image with overlaid white copy) and the right card is a smaller photo above a copy block on a gray bg. Sits behind the hero's 32px rounded bottom corners — `margin-top: -32px` so the gray reveals. Parallax-lifts as the user scrolls.
- **App carousel ("More than tools").** Section padding 128px top and bottom, eyebrow-headline-deck-CTA stack centered, then a horizontal carousel of `.app-card`s left-aligned to the grid's column 1. Card width JS-synced to grid measurement. Dot navigation centered, 8px dots inactive `#d0d0d0`, active scaled 1.25× and `#000`. Round arrows on either side.
- **Perks 4-up.** 4-column grid of `.perk-card`s. Section padding 128px. At ≤1023px collapses to 2 columns; at ≤767px stacks to 1.
- **Hub-router accordion (alternative hero).** Used on the Hub page variant. A 4–5 card flex row where one card is `--active`, expanding from 291px to ~491px and crossfading to `#000` ground with white type and a 48×48 white-plus CTA. Adjacent cards stay at 291px in `#f6f6f6`. Animation timing `--hhub-duration: 600ms`, `--hhub-ease: cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`.

### Sticky CTA Pill
A bottom-center floating element that morphs from a 279×56 pill into a full pricing modal. Pill state shows an icon + "Start free trial" label. Tap expands the container width, height, and inner content via GSAP. Three plan-cards display side-by-side on desktop, scroll-snap horizontally on mobile. Toggle button at bottom-center returns to pill state. Backdrop scrim activates at modal-open with `rgba(0,0,0,0.6)` + 12px blur.

### Footer
Multi-column footer (5 cols on desktop, 6 on widest, stacking mobile-side). `background: #000`, white type. Sections include product list, company list, support, legal, social. Footer carries a large wordmark treatment that clip-path reveals upward as it scrolls into view (60% inset → 0%). Legal row at the bottom with adchoices and CCPA marks.

## 6. Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
- **Do** lead every section with an eyebrow at 16/700/-0.01em above the Title 2 headline. The eyebrow is the category cue; without it the page reads as anonymous.
- **Do** use Adobe Clean Display Black (900) at 80–96px on the hero. Anything lighter looks like a different brand.
- **Do** use 16px as the default radius — cards, mega-nav panel bottom, mosaic tiles, product cards. Use 24px only on app-carousel cards. Use 32px only on full-bleed section bottom corners.
- **Do** rotate exactly two grays for surfaces: `#f8f8f8` (mega-nav, perks, plans) and `#f6f6f6` (editorial, app card, hub-router).
- **Do** pin the hero for 220vh+ and scrub the mosaic convergence to scroll progress with `scrub: 0.8`.
- **Do** apply the global Text Animator to every section headline using `[data-ta]` and `[data-ta-group]`.
- **Do** vendor GSAP, ScrollTrigger, and Lenis locally — these are required, not optional.
- **Do** crossfade the gnav pill from invisible to frosted-glass at >40px scroll, with the logo and link colors flipping in lockstep.

### Don't:
- **Don't** add a `box-shadow` to the gnav. Use frosted glass (`backdrop-filter: blur(64px)` + `rgba(255,255,255,0.51)`) instead — that's the depth metaphor.
- **Don't** spread the trial-blue (#3b63fb) onto links, eyebrows, nav, or feature accents. It is reserved for the trial CTA. Anywhere else, primary CTAs are ink-dark on white.
- **Don't** introduce a third gray. Anything that isn't `#f8f8f8` or `#f6f6f6` belongs as `#fff` (card) or `#000` (active state).
- **Don't** use rounded-rectangle buttons. All CTAs are pill-shaped (75px or 999px). A 6px or 8px-radius button signals the wrong system.
- **Don't** use clamp() on Title sizes. The system swaps fixed tokens at the breakpoint media queries (96→72→56 for Super; 80→56→40 for Title 1). Token-swap-by-breakpoint is the contract.
- **Don't** load `ScrollSmoother.min.js`. It collapses document height to 0 and breaks scroll. Use Lenis for smooth scroll, GSAP+ScrollTrigger for pinning.
- **Don't** add badge-soup, gradient hero backgrounds, or "social proof" logo strips beneath the hero. The hero is one frame: copy, mosaic, two CTAs.
- **Don't** use the Adobe red (#eb1000) anywhere except the wordmark. It is not a status color.
- **Don't** introduce `text-transform: uppercase` on body or eyebrow copy. The system uses sentence case throughout — uppercase reads as a different design language.
- **Don't** use `box-shadow` on cards as a default lift. Cards live on tonal layering. Reach for shadow only on photographic content where the lift is intentional (the editorial right-card photo shadow).

## 7. Accessibility — reduced motion

The system is motion-led but motion is not load-bearing for comprehension. When `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` is set, the page must still resolve to its final visual state — copy visible, mosaic in its tight grid, sticky pill in place — without scroll-pinned timelines, scrubbed reveals, or clip-path morphs.

**Collapse rules.**

- **Hero mosaic-converge** → static. Skip the 220vh pin and the spread→tight ScrollTrigger timeline. Render the mosaic in its final tight (0,0) positions on load with a 200ms opacity fade-in. No scrub, no pinning.
- **Text Animator scroll-scrub reveals** → instant. `[data-ta]` and `[data-ta-group]` lines render at full opacity and `y:0` on first paint; no scrubbed timeline. An optional ≤150ms fade is acceptable but not required.
- **Hub-router accordion** → instant flex-basis. Active card sizes to its expanded width on selection without the 600ms cubic-bezier interpolation. State change is allowed; the transition duration is 0.
- **Sticky CTA pill → modal morph** → instant. The pill expands to modal on tap without the GSAP scale/radius interpolation. Scrim appears at `rgba(0,0,0,0.6)` immediately (no backdrop-blur fade-in).
- **Mega-nav clip-path expand** → instant. Skip the 0.48s `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` clip-path interpolation; show the expanded panel directly.
- **Footer wordmark clip reveal** → static. Render the wordmark at its final 0% inset on first paint; do not animate the upward reveal.
- **Lenis smooth scroll** → disabled. Native scroll only. Lenis self-disables under `prefers-reduced-motion`; verify the prototype honors this rather than overriding it.

**What stays.** Hover states, focus rings, button-press depression, and any state-change transition ≤200ms remain — these are interactive feedback, not presentational motion.

**Authoring rule.** Prototypes must guard any GSAP timeline / ScrollTrigger registration with a `matchMedia('(prefers-reduced-motion: no-preference)')` check so the timelines simply don't initialize when the user has opted out. Static fallbacks must produce the same final composition — never a stripped-down or text-only page.

## Font availability note

**Decision.** This DESIGN spec assumes Adobe Clean and Adobe Clean Display are available via the Adobe-licensed font path used in `designs/hub/styles/global/typography.css` and `designs/bizpro-hub-prototype (1)/bizpro-hub.html` — both reference the same OTF files in `assets/fonts/`. Downstream prototypes and the migrated site should `@font-face`-load the same files when the license-bearing build path is intact.

**Fallback for non-license builds.** If the prototype is rendered outside the license-bearing context (e.g., a public preview site or an external sandbox), substitute **Source Sans 3** (Adobe's open-source sans, the closest available proportion to Adobe Clean) for the body face and **Source Sans 3** at weight 900 plus a `font-stretch: condensed` cue (or, if available, **Inter** weight 900 with negative letter-spacing aggressively tightened to `-0.04em`) for the display face. Source Sans 3 is preferred because it shares Adobe Clean's humanist proportions and ships in weight 900; Inter is a second choice when Source Sans isn't available because its 900 cut is denser than typical Helvetica-family sans and tracks letter-spacing well at display sizes.

**Trade-off.** Source Sans 3 at 900 is narrower than Adobe Clean Display Black; the headline gestures will read as roughly 5% lighter. To compensate, increase the negative letter-spacing on Title 1 from `-0.04em` to `-0.045em` and reduce line-height from `0.96` to `0.94`. The fallback never matches the licensed look — but it preserves the structural rhythm. Document the substitution in the prototype's provenance comment.
